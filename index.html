About Us

What The Craft is a weekend event that is held during the summer month of August. Gathering all beer lovers alike to share and experience everything beer while offering good food and music to inspiring people!
With Vancouver offering over 40 breweries, it is only natural to hold an event to appreciate the love for breweries at the heart of Vancouver. With this event, we hope to bring new and returning people to try local breweries and taste what Vancouver has to offer! Our goal with What The Craft is to allow local breweries to come together and collaborate a movement in BC’s craft beer culture. Purchase your tickets to join the craft movement and to start supporting your local breweries!
